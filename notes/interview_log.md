## Error taxonomy

- **Concept:** did not know the idea.
- **Implementation:** knew the idea but could not code it.
- **Debugging:** missed a shape, index, mask, mutation, or numerical-stability issue.
- **Communication:** had the right solution but unclear structure.
- **Judgment:** did not explain trade-offs, evaluation, or limitations.

Only track one primary error per attempt. Re-test it within 72 hours.
