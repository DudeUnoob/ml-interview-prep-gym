# Math, Probability, and Derivation Packet

## Probability

1. Derive expectation and variance for Bernoulli, Binomial, and Gaussian variables.
2. Explain conditional probability, Bayes rule, independence, covariance, and correlation.
3. Given an imbalanced classifier, explain precision, recall, AUROC, PR-AUC, calibration, and threshold choice.
4. Derive why averaging independent noisy estimates reduces variance.
5. Design a statistically valid A/B test; state assumptions and common traps.

## Linear algebra

1. Derive the shape and complexity of QK^T attention for B, T, H, D.
2. Explain eigenvectors/eigenvalues, SVD, PCA, rank, and conditioning.
3. Explain why normalization and initialization affect gradient flow.

## Calculus / optimization

1. Derive gradients for MSE, sigmoid+BCE, and softmax cross-entropy.
2. Explain chain rule through a two-layer MLP.
3. Compare SGD, momentum, RMSProp, and Adam.
4. Explain convexity, local minima, saddle points, and learning-rate schedules.

## Answer format

State definitions, write dimensions, derive one line at a time, then give intuition and an ML use case.
