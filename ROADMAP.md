# 12 Week ML Interview Curriculum

## Phase 1: Foundations
Week 1: Arrays, Hashing, Two Pointers, Stack
Week 2: Trees, Graphs, DFS/BFS, Backtracking
Week 3: Probability, Linear Algebra, Calculus

## Phase 2: ML Coding
Week 4: Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, Gradient Descent
Week 5: Backpropagation, BatchNorm, Optimizers
Week 6: PyTorch Fluency

## Phase 3: Transformers
Week 7: Attention, Positional Encoding
Week 8: Decoding, KV Cache, Tokenization
Week 9: RLHF, PPO, GRPO

## Phase 4: Systems + Research
Week 10: Distributed Training, FSDP, Tensor Parallelism
Week 11: Experiment Design + Research Discussions
Week 12: Behavioral + Mock Interviews

Target companies: OpenAI, Anthropic, NVIDIA, Tesla AI, Amazon AI, Applied Scientist Internships, Quant Dev roles.
