# Behavioral Story Bank

Build these as 90-second STAR answers plus a 30-second version. Keep claims truthful, concrete, and public-safe.

| Question family | Best starting experience | Result / lesson to prepare |
|---|---|---|
| Ownership under ambiguity | Colicit or early-stage project work | How you turned an unclear user/problem signal into a scoped plan |
| Deep dive / debugging | Frigate, FleetWatch, or Amazon-safe generic example | Your debugging tree, instrumentation, and root cause |
| Disagreement | Team research / startup collaboration | How you surfaced evidence, changed your mind or aligned the group |
| Failure | A model/experiment/product iteration that did not work | What hypothesis failed and how you changed process |
| Customer obsession | Recipe Notes / Tree-Plenish / product work | Feedback loop and metric you moved |
| Bias for action | Startup or incident-response work | Smallest reversible decision and risk control |
| Learning fast | Research pivot / new system or ML concept | How you built a mental model and validated it |
| High standards | Research validation or production reliability work | What quality bar you enforced and why |
| Ethical judgment | Healthcare / archival AI work | Privacy, bias, interpretability, or human-in-the-loop decision |

## STAR quality bar

- **Situation:** one sentence, no long background.
- **Task:** your exact responsibility and constraint.
- **Action:** the decisions *you* made; include alternatives rejected.
- **Result:** measurable outcome, then a short reflection.

## Amazon add-on

After each story, map it to one Leadership Principle and prepare one follow-up on a trade-off, failure mode, or stakeholder reaction.
